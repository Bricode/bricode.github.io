Post sprint one reflection

Group reflection

What worked well? Independently worked efficiently, we had a functional product to show the client, majority of tasks were completed and communication was good
What could be improved? Merging earlier to give us more time to fix merging conflicts to make work cohesive. Could of made more merges to the master instead of one big one at the end of the sprint
What will we commit to doing in the next Sprint? Continuing good communication, completing tasks in decent time frames and not straying off path from what the client envisions.
